StreamCam Privacy Policy

Your Data

This policy describes how your personal data is treated in StreamCam for iOS.

Data Collection

StreamCam does not collect or have access to any personal data on your device. Live camera footage is not recorded or stored, and only appears on your device screen, which is then mirrored to your Mac using Apple's screen recording functionality.

Contact

Please email streamcam@icloud.com if you would like to discuss this policy further.
