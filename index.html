StreamCam

StreamCam provides a simple, uncluttered camera viewer that lets you use your iOS camera with your Mac.

Record to your Mac

StreamCam takes advantage of the built in screen sharing functionality between iOS and macOS.

Connect your device to your Mac via lightening cable and select it as a source in Quicktime. It provides you with a no-fuss, unobstructed view of your iOS camera that can be used in a variety of use cases.
