StreamCam provides a simple, uncluttered camera viewer that lets you use your iOS camera with your Mac.

### Record to your Mac Connect your device to your Mac via lightening cable and select it as a source in Quicktime. ### Stream Connect your device to your Mac via lightening cable and select it as a source in a streaming app such as OBS or Ecamm Live. ### Webcam Use OBS or Ecamm Live plugins to create a virtual camera device which you can select as a source in Zoom, Skype, etc. ### Don't forget! Turn off your notifications or turn on airplane mode to avoid any embarassing notifications while streaming!
